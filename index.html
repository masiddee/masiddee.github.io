Sudi-do A simple and easy task list

Sudi-do

A simple task list. Sudi-do makes it easy to keep track of your current tasks to ensure things get done. Simply type your task into the textbox and hit enter, Sudi-do will append the task to the end of the list. You can even go in and edit tasks you've already created.

Technologies used: HTML5/CSS3, jQuery
